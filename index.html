Bored of the same old events? Glastonbury - more like Glastonboring?
Check out the map below to see some interesting events you may not have heard of or considered going to before. From snail racing in Norfolk, to air balloons in Bristol, here are our top recommended quirky events in the UK.

Click on each pin on the map to see more.


Cheese Rolling, Gloucester
The Cooper's Hill Cheese Rolling is an annual event held on the Spring Bank Holiday near Gloucester. It involves chasing a 9lb Double Gloucester Cheese down the near vertical slopes of the hill. It is offically an extreme sport and attracts many spectators every year.
Summer Solstice, Stonehenge
The Summer Solstice is the most important day of the year at Stonehenge and it's a magical time to be there.
Air Balloon Festival, Bristol
Europe's largest annual meeting of hot air balloons, attracting over 130 Hot Air Balloons from across the globe.
Viking Festival, York
Celebrate York's Norse heritage at the JORVIK Viking Festival 2020 with reenactments, battles and Viking history.
Snail racing, Norfolk
Watch more than 200 snails compete in the World Snail Racing Championships held at Congham, in Norfolk.
Cirque du Soleil, Royal Albert Hall London
Cirque du Soleil returns to London's Royal Albert Hall in 2020, bringing its wonderful spectacle, LUZIA to Europe for the first time.

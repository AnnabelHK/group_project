Air Balloon Festival, Bristol

6 Aug 2020 - 9 Aug 2020



What is it?

Over 100 hot air balloons of all shapes, colours and sizes take to the skies with beautiful Bristol providing an incredibly photogenic backdrop. It attracts hundreds of thousands of people each year.

When is it?

The Bristol Air Balloon Festival takes place each August, and usually runs for 4 days over a weekend. The dates change each year so be sure to check the official website here. The 2020 Bristol Balloon Fiesta is scheduled from August 6th to August 9th.

Where is it held?

All the main events take place within a section of the Ashton Court Estate in Bristol, England. There are several car parks for the event and you should follow signs to park once you get near the park.

How much is admission?

Admission is FREE! It is one of the largest free events in the UK and Europe. However, you do have to pay for parking if you choose to drive.

Viking festival, York

15 Feb 2020 - 23 Feb 2020



What is it?

The annual JORVIK Viking Festival is a city-wide celebration of York's Norse heritage, run by York Archaeological Trust.

When is it?

It takes place for a week in mid February. In 2020, it will happen between 15th and 23rd February. Take a look at the official website here to find out about what's on this year.

Where is it held?

With a large amount of different events across several days, the locations vary, but they are usually somewhere in central York.

How much is admission?

At JORVIK Viking Festival, some events have a fee and are ticketed, and some are free and unticketed.
